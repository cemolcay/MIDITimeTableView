  • virtualizationRect

    The x-range (in this view’s own coordinate space) of bars to realize layers for. Bars outside it are freed into a reuse pool instead of laid out, so the number of live MIDITimeTableMeasureLayers tracks what’s on screen rather than the whole timeline. The owning MIDITimeTableView keeps this in sync with the visible viewport (plus a small overscan margin) on every layout pass, including scroll. Defaults to a large placeholder so a view used standalone, before anything sets a real value, still shows its bars.
